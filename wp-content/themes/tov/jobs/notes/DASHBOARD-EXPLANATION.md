### **Add New Job**
- Click "Add New Job" button
- Creates a new job posting
- Redirects to job editor

### **Manage Jobs**
- Click "Manage Jobs" button
- Goes to standard WordPress job management
- Shows all jobs with bulk actions

- Click "Refresh Overview" button

## How to Use Your Dashboard

### **Daily Management**:
1. **Check Statistics**: Look at the top cards for quick overview
2. **Review Applications**: Check recent applications in the middle section
3. **Manage Job Status**: Use the status toggles to show/hide jobs
4. **Monitor Performance**: Track application counts per job

### **Job Status Management**:
1. **To Hide a Job**: Click the green "ACTIVE" badge ‚Üí it turns red "INACTIVE"
2. **To Show a Job**: Click the red "INACTIVE" badge ‚Üí it turns green "ACTIVE"
3. **Verify Changes**: Check your website to confirm jobs appear/disappear

### **Application Management**:
1. **View Applications**: Check the Recent Applications table
2. **Download Resumes**: Click "View Resume" buttons
3. **Contact Applicants**: Use email addresses to reach out
4. **Track Performance**: See application counts per job
